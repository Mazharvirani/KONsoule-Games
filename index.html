ABOUT KONSOUL GAMES

Corporate Headquarters
DHA SUFFA,KARACHI
 Founded in 2023, konsoul Games is an pakistani company founded by CEO MAZHAR. The company is headquartered in DHA SUFFA,KARACHI and has more than 40 offices worldwide. Today konsoul is a leading interactive entertainment company and provider of 3D engine technology. konsoul operates Fortnite, one of the world’s largest games with over 350 million accounts and 2.5 billion friend connections. konsoul also develops Unreal Engine, which powers the world’s leading games and is also adopted across industries such as film and television, architecture, automotive, manufacturing, and simulation. Through Unreal Engine, konsoul Games Store, and konsoul Online Services, konsoul provides an end-to-end digital ecosystem for developers and creators to build, distribute, and operate games and other content.

frequently aksed questions

Can I have a studio tour?
 We do not offer studio tours as a general policy. While we would love to give you the full konsoul experience, we’re committed to providing the most conducive working environment for our folks so they can stay focused and keep making great games and technology.
Does konsoul have any job openings?
 Yes! Our current openings will be posted on our career portal. Once registered, you may apply for open positions and/or submit your resume.
How do I learn more about konsoul?
 For an overview of konsoul visit our careers page and follow us on LinkedIn. For information regarding the Transparency in Coverage rule for the US, click here.
Do you offer internships?
 Any summer jobs or internships will be posted on our career portal.
I am having trouble with my game. Where do I go for support?
 Please visit help.konsoulgames.com for support on all of our current products. For classic games in the Unreal series, visit help.unrealtournament.com.
What about Gears of War?
 Microsoft purchased the Gears of War IP from konsoul Games, and they own the franchise now. For the Gears of War series, contact Xbox Support.
